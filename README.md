# 📊 Business Analysis of Hotel Bookings

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing hotel booking data to uncover key business insights, identify patterns in customer behavior, and provide actionable recommendations to improve revenue, reduce cancellations, and optimize booking strategies.

The analysis is performed using Python in Google Colab, with a strong emphasis on business-oriented thinking and data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
The primary objectives of this analysis are:

- Identify key trends and patterns in hotel bookings  
- Analyze cancellation behavior and its underlying causes  
- Evaluate performance across booking channels, room types, and property ratings  
- Provide actionable recommendations to improve profitability and customer retention  

---

## 🗂️ Dataset Description
The dataset contains booking-level information including:

- Customer & Property Details (`customer_id`, `property_id`, `city`)  
- Booking Information (`booking_date`, `check_in_date`, `check_out_date`)  
- Property Attributes (`room_type`, `star_rating`)  
- Pricing Data (`costprice`, `selling_price`, `markup`, `booking_value`)  
- Booking Behavior (`booking_status`, `channel_of_booking`, `payment_method`)  
- Promotional Data (`cashback`, `coupon_redeem`)

## 📊 Key Insights & Findings

### 1. Booking Trends
- Certain booking channels dominate overall bookings, indicating dependency on specific platforms.  
- Room type analysis shows higher demand for budget-friendly accommodations.  
- Higher star-rated hotels generate greater revenue per booking.

### 2. Cancellation Behavior
- A significant percentage of bookings are cancelled, affecting revenue stability.  
- Cancellation rates vary across channels and customer segments.  
- Price-sensitive customers are more likely to cancel bookings.

### 3. Seasonal Patterns
- Booking demand fluctuates across months, indicating peak and off-peak seasons.  
- Stay duration increases during high-demand periods.

---

## 🔍 Root Cause Analysis

- **Price Sensitivity:** Customers tend to cancel when better deals are available.  
- **Channel Influence:** Third-party platforms attract low-commitment users.  
- **Payment Flexibility:** Lack of upfront payment increases cancellations.  
- **Property Trust:** Lower-rated properties experience higher cancellation rates.  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 🔹 Reduce Cancellations
- Introduce partial advance payments  
- Restrict free cancellation windows  
- Incentivize non-refundable bookings  

### 🔹 Improve Profitability
- Optimize pricing and markup strategies  
- Focus on high-margin properties  
- Control excessive discounts and cashback  

### 🔹 Increase Repeat Bookings
- Implement loyalty programs  
- Provide personalized recommendations  
- Enhance customer experience  

### 🔹 Optimize Channel Strategy
- Focus on high-performing channels  
- Reduce dependency on high-cancellation sources
